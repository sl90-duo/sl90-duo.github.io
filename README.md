Short Bio: Rewind Bens
The Music You Remember, the Way You’ve Never Heard It.

Rewind Bens is an acoustic duo born from a simple weekly ritual: two friends, two guitars, and a shared obsession with the hits that defined the 80s, 90s, and 2000s. Named after the shared last names of members Roey and Peer, the duo brings a fresh, organic energy to the soundtracks of our lives.

Their setlist is a curated trip down memory lane, stripping back everything from the haunting melodies of Radiohead’s "High and Dry" and Duran Duran’s "Ordinary World" to the high-energy anthems of Britney Spears and Imagine Dragons.

Whether you’re looking to grab a drink and get lost in the vocal harmonies of "Fast Car" or find yourself singing along to "Patience," Rewind Bens delivers a performance that bridges the gap between an intimate unplugged session and a lively sing-along.

Come for the nostalgia. Stay for the chemistry.
